1932 Ford Truck

In 2010 this 1932 Ford had been selected for the Keystone Automotive Industries Inc. calendar. This truck also made 2006’s October issue of Rodder’s Digest as well as the August issue of Goodguys. It was selected by Street Rodder Magazine as one of the top 100 “street rods” of the year in 2007. After all votes were placed, this truck came in third over all for street rod of the year.
