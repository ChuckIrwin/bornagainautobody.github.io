Born Again Auto Body

Born Again Auto Body is the family owned business of
car enthusiasts Chuck and Karen Irwin. Their business officially opened its doors to the public in April of 2003, offering services done by trained professionals who take
great pride in their work, which specializes in show cars
and collision repairs. Some of their work has even been featured in magazines and calendars, as well as winning awards for their restoration on classic cars.
